The Elements of Art

This webpage will teach you all about the elements of art! From what they are to how the look, the information provided will help improve your understandings of art.

What Is Art?

Art is a way of expressing the artist's emotion and conveying it to an audience. It can be something visual, like a painting or a dance, or something you can sense like music or the taste of a cake.

Elements

Name Definition Example
Line
  • A continuous mark made on a surface by a moving point
  • It can convey movement, emotions, be thick or thin, curvey or straight
Shape
  • A closed 2-D space that has height and width but no depth
  • It can be defined by other elements of art
  • It two basic forms, geometric and organic.
Form
  • A shape that is now 3-D, having depth rather than just having height and width
Space
  • Space is the area withi, around, above, or below an object
  • It can be divided into 3 parts: foreground, middleground, and background
  • As well as provides perspective into pieces of art
Texture
  • The way the surface of an object feels, it can be implied or real
Value
  • The range of lightness and darkness a color has, it allows for objects to appear real
Color
  • The way our brain perceives the reflection of light
  • There are three primary colors: red, yellow, and blue
  • 3 properties: hue, intensity, and value
